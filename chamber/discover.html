El Malecon, located on the coast of Santo Domingo, is an excellent place to hang out with the family and on top of that, to have food businesses for everyone.

The Mirador Park, located in the Cacicazgos, is a good place for a rich rest and is frequented by people, in which activities are carried out to promote and help companies grow.

The place is called the Plaza de la Bandera in Spanish (Plaza de la Bandera), it is in the center of the city, a very crowded place for vehicles and a tourist place, a good place to advertise.

Santo Domingo Temple, located on Calle Bolivar, with a beautiful view, around it is the Parque de las Luces and several stores that can be of great help to you.

Agora Mall is one of the largest Malls in Santo Domingo, where most people go, where they enjoy themselves and work, in the immensity of businesses that exist.
