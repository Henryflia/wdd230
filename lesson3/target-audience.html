Description of the Targer Audience

  • Who: "People who are interested in growing their businesses and improving the community"
  • Age: "18 to 40"
  • Accessability: "Laptos, desktop, tablets and smartphones"

Persona

Persona 1 "Jose is 25 years old, he has just started his new grocery store, he wants to be able to grow in this way since he is new to this and very few people know him, he believes that through the networks he can achieve it, but he needs help"

Persona 2 "Nereida, she has a company and wants to grow, she wants to work on it but she doesn't know how to start, in addition to the fact that there aren't many people on the side where she is and it is difficult for her to be known"

Five Listed Scenarios

  1. How can I promote my company?
  2. What medium should I use for people to know me?
  3. What should I do to make my company grow?
  4. Where can I learn these things by myself?
  5. What should I do to get people to come to my store?
